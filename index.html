About the Syrian Refugee Heatmap

Our heatmap shows the movement of families due to forced migration from Syria. This tool allows representation of three dimensions of data: the location of refugees, the amount of refugees, and a timestamp. Moving the slider advances the timeline.

This requires some heavy duty HTML5 features and works best in Google Chrome. We disable certain features on mobile devices and other browsers. Use of this tool was made possible through David-Hamp Gonsalves' Halifax Crime Heat Map (c) 2015.

We can only pack so much awesome into your mobile device, visit on a computer for the full effect.

By Michael McRoskey
